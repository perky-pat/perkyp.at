Fan Beat
Designed by Studio Output (London) and Field.io (London), Fan Beat is an installation for concerts based on a wall projection and a large touch screen. The installation visualizes the social interactions as generative creatures (metaballs) during the concert.

WebGL, Three.js, javascript, data visualisation (D3.js).
