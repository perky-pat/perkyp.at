Bio


My name is Esteban. I explore visual arts and computational design, mostly through front end, web technologies.
In 2005 I graduated in art and cinema studies at the University of Bologna, Italy. Today I create interactive applications in collaboration with creative studios, designers and artists.
I am a freelancer based in London.

Tech

If you have a project and wonder if I could help, these are the tools and techniques I normally use: Javascript, Three.js / WebGL, canvas 2D, D3.js, Node, most typical tools (Babel, Webpack, Gulp, etc) and some frameworks (Backbone and React).
