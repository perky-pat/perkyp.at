Agency: Signal Noise
Pride & Prejudice 2016
Designed by Signal Noise, this application shows survey data collected by The Economist, for an international event in support of LGBT rights: Pride & Prejudice.
The interesting part of the work is a long interactive streamgraph that spans across several screens.
